Jummu and Kashmir

The natural beauty of Jammu and Kashmir, as well as its wonderful climate, are well-known. This is India's greatest and most well-known tourist attraction. Kashmir must be visited during the winter season, being the most popular season for visitors. Sonamarg, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Yusmarg, Charar-i-Sharief, Sinthan top, Vaishno Devi, and Patni top should all be on your itinerary. The most prominent festivals are Lohri and Baisakhi. Pulaos are a popular dish in this area. The location is accessible through a national highway, and you have various transportation options to get there.
